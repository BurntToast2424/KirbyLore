Lore Hub

This website will not cover the entirety of the lore in the series. It will only cover the following:

  • The Ancients
  • Dark Matter, and it's various forms
  • Kirby, and what he is
  • The Butterfly of Paradise, Morpho Knight

It also only covers the American version, as it is localized compared to Japan. I haven't played every single Kirby game myself, so I had to use other sources. The following videos by TheRpgMonger on YouTube were very helpful for this.
