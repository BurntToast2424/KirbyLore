Dimensions vs Timelines

There are times where you will hear something called a Dimension, but there are two things this might be refering to. I have made a table to explain the differences between the things often called a dimension in game, and alternate timelines in the lore.

Dimensions Timelines
Generally refered to as "canon" Generally refered to as "not canon"
Kirby and other beings can travel between them Nothing can travel between them*
Show up in the main story Only show up in subgames

*The only exception is the Butterfly of Paradise.
